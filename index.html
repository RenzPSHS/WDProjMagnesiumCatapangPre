"Feed your Mind, One Book at a Time"

Snakademic is a fresh spin on the classic snake game, designed to blend fast-paced arcade fun with an academic twist. Players guide their snake across a grid collecting books that makes the snake longer, but before that, they need to answer a question correctly to earn each book' and make the snake grow. The game challenges players' reflexes and knowledge, making it an engaging way to learn while playing.

There is point system that keeps track of the player's score based on the number of books collected and questions answered correctly. Some books make the snake shorter, allowing players to continuesly gain points for the leaderboard. Different speeds can be picked to increase or decrease the difficuly to the users preference. Snakademic offers a unique blend of education and entertainment, making learning fun and interactive.
