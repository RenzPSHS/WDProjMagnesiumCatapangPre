This game is designed to help you study while keeping your hands busy. The objective is simple: use the arrow keys or WASD to move the snake, collect apples to grow, and earn points while answering the questions that appear. On the bottom of the screen, a multiple‑choice question will be displayed, and you’ll have 15 seconds to answer it.

If you answer correctly, you’ll gain double points and extra length for each apple collected over the next 10 seconds. For basic challenges, the questions will focus on arithmetic. However, if you get the answer wrong, the snake will move at double speed for 10 seconds, adding an extra layer of difficulty to the game.

"Remember, practice makes perfect! The more you play, the better you'll get at both the game and your arithmetic skills. Good Luck, and have fun!"
