This game is made to help you study whilst also being able to keep your hands busy. The goal is simple, use arrow keys, or WASD to move the snake, grow and get score while answering the questions being given to you. On the right side of the scree there will be a multiple choice question that you have to answer within 15 seconds. If you answer the question correctly, then you will get double score and length for each apple for 10 seconds. If you get it wrong you will move at double speed for 10 seconds. Good Luck, and have fun!
